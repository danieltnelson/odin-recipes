Tostadas

Descrption

This recipe is a super simple version for those of you who like to spend about 5 minutes preparing dinner after work.

Ingredients

  • Tostadas
  • Refried beans
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Cholula or any other hot sauce

Steps

  1. Heat up your refried beans on medium heat.
  2. Once they're warmed up you're ready to prepare.
  3. Just slap as much beans as you can on the tostada, add lettuce and cheese, then top it with hot sauce.
  4. That's it! Eat as many as you'd like!
