Frito Pie

Descrption

Frito Pie is a simple and delicious staple in my household. There are a few ways to make it. I'll show you the most recent one I've learned.

Ingredients

  • Fritos
  • White Rice
  • Lettuce
  • Ranch style beans
  • Cheese
  • Cholula or any other hot sauce

Steps

  1. Boil some water and make your white rice first.
  2. Add your beans to a pot and keep it on low.
  3. Once the rice is done, you're ready to put all the ingredients in a bowl.
  4. I start with a bed of fritos, then rice, beans, cheese (to melt on the warm beans), lettuce, and hot sauce.
  5. Done. An even easier version is to just use fritos, beans, and cheese.
